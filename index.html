Pasta receipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Red chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Kasuri methi
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Green peas
  • Capsicum

Quantity

Ingredients Quantity
pasta 2cup
olive oil 2 table spoon
cummin seeds 1/2 teaspoon
garlic 2 cloves
salt 1/3 table spoon
Red chilli powder 1/2 teaspoon
Garam masala 1/2 teaspoon
Kasuri methi 1/2 teaspoon
Cream 2 table spoon
Onion 1 large Onion(finely chopped)
Tomato 1 tomato(chopped)
Carrot 2
green peas 1/2 cup
Capsicum 1/4 cup

Preparation

In a large pot, bring 6 cups of water to a boil.
When the water begins to boil rapidly, add half
teaspoon salt and pasta. Cook till all dente on
a medium to high flame.I suggest referring the
instructions on the pack of pasta. While the pasta
cooks, rinse and chop the veggies.drain off the
pasta to a colander when done and rinse in cold water.
Set aside. reserve 3 to 4 tablespoons of pasta cooked water.

How to make

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan. When the oil turns hot, add cumin.
  2. When they begin to splutter, then add finely chopped garlic.
  3. Fry till it begins to smell good for a minute or two.
  4. Add onions and fry until they turn pink.
  5. Then add chopped veggies ad fry for about 2 minuts.
  6. Next mash the tomatoes well and add it to the pan along with salt.
  7. cook until the tomatoes well and add it to the pan along with salt.
  8. Cook untill the tomatoes turn soft and blend will with veggies. The
    raw smell of tomatoes should disappear.
  9. Add kasuri methi, red chili powder and masala powder.
  10. Stir well and fry for a minute. switch off the stove. If using cream
    add now and stir.
  11. Add cooked pasta and 1 tablespoon oil. toss well. adjust salt as needed.
  12. Add coriander leaves, If the pasta looks dry, then pour 1 tp 2 tablespoons
    pasta cooked water.
  13. Serve masala pasta hot.
